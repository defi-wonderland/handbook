# Getting Paid

We use **Deel** to manage contracts, payments, and compliance. It supports contributors and employees around the world and gives you flexibility in how you receive funds.

You’ll get paid on the **last business day of each month**.

## What Deel Handles

- Contracts
- Payments (bank, crypto, wallets)
- Tax documentation and history

Once your account is set up, you can view invoices, change payment methods, and track status directly in your dashboard.

## How to Set Up Deel

1. **Create your Deel account** using your **personal email**

:::tip IMPORTANT
**DON'T** use your Wonderland email, this account stays with you long-term.
:::

2. When you’re added to the Wonderland workspace:
   - You’ll receive an invite to sign your contract
   - Fill in your personal details and payment preferences
   - Choose your preferred withdrawal method  
     (bank, Wise, Payoneer, or crypto — this can be changed any time)

3. **Complete KYC** (identity verification), this is required in most countries

Once everything is approved, you’re all set. Payments will be processed automatically at the end of each month.

## Security and Flexibility

- Deel is **GDPR-compliant**
- You can **switch payout methods** at any time
- You’ll receive emails about payments, invoices, and compliance, keep an eye on your inbox.

# Security & Access

We take security seriously, because we want everyone to work confidently, without risk to themselves or the team. These tools help us keep credentials safe and accounts protected.

## 1Password: credential management

We use **1Password** to manage work-related logins, API keys, and sensitive information. It’s end-to-end encrypted, and data is never visible to admins or stored in plain text. If you already use your own password manager, that’s fine. But if not, you’ll get access to a **Wonderland-sponsored 1Password Family account**, a personal vault that the company doesn’t control or monitor.

### How to Set Up Your Personal 1Password Vault

1. **Log in to** the Wonderland workspace:  
   [https://wonderland.1password.com/home](https://wonderland.1password.com/home)

2. In the bottom-right corner, find and click:  
   **“Claim your free family account”**

3. Set up your **personal vault** using a **personal email** (not your @defi.sucks or @wonderland.xyz)

4. If you already have data in the Wonderland vault:
   - Select all items
   - Move them to your personal vault
   - Log out from the Wonderland workspace

5. Enable **2FA** on your personal account

Once that’s done, use your personal vault for all future storage. Avoid using the Wonderland workspace going forward.

## Gmail: company email

Your Wonderland Gmail account is used for:
- Internal announcements
- Calendar invites
- Tool access and notifications

You must enable **two-factor authentication**. If 2FA is missing, your account will be locked after a few days.

Make sure you keep access to this inbox and monitor it for alerts, especially related to Float and scheduling.
